Scott Lobenberg is the founder of JobFairX and has spent more than 20 years in the recruiting and hiring events industry. He built JobFairX around one idea: hiring channels should deliver scheduled interviews, not application queues — candidates pick the employer, self-schedule the interview, and show up 91% of the time.

Today the platform runs interview-first virtual hiring events across healthcare, technology, veteran, diversity, and entry-level hiring. Scott writes here about what the event data shows: recruiting benchmarks, cost per hire, show rates, and how hospitals and high-volume employers actually fill roles.
