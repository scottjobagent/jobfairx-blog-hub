Healthcare job fair FAQ

Are these healthcare job fairs virtual or in person?

Virtual. Every event on this calendar runs online, so your team interviews from anywhere — no booth, no travel, no venue costs. Local candidate pools are built per metro.

How is this different from a traditional job fair?

Candidates don't wander a room. They're screened for credentials at registration, request your organization specifically, and self-schedule interviews before the event. You get a calendar of scheduled interviews, not a stack of resumes — and 91% of scheduled candidates show up.

What healthcare roles do these events cover?

Registered nurses, CNAs and aides, medical assistants, allied health, behavioral health, and clinical support. Use the role filter above to find events matching your openings.

What does a healthcare hiring event cost?

Events are flat-rate with interviews included — cost per hire falls as you hire more from one event. Full details on the pricing page.

Looking for a healthcare job? These events are always 100% free for job seekers.
